Math

When \(a \ne 0\), there are two solutions to \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) and they are:

$$x = {-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac} \over 2a}. $$

See MathMultiline

Digits that immediately follow a \$ sign will not start an inline math spa so prices like $50 etc. don't have to be escaped!
